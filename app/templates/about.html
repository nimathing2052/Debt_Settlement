About ClearSkies

Behind the scenes, we are seven students in the Master of Data Science for Public Policy program at the Hertie School of Governance in Berlin, Germany. As part of our Data Structures and Algorithms class, we were tasked with creating, implementing and testing our own web application. Often, as students, we face debt settlement issues in shared flats, on group trips or just in everyday situations with friends. This app has been developed to simplify the user's financial management experience.

As you explore our platform, you'll find intuitive features such as adding users, creating groups, managing expenses with automated splitting, and effortlessly tracking your overall transactions through our Personal and Overall Dashboards.

Get ready to experience debt settlement like never before!


How it Works

Our goal is to offer you a simplified experience of solving any financial troubles you might have.

With the help of ClearSkies, you can easily keep track of your finances! Whether you're looking to add a new debt, settle up with a friend, or simply check your financial overview, we've got you covered!

Follow the steps below and we'll take care of the rest:

  • Log in or register a new account.
  • In the "finances" menu, create a new group together with other users.
  • Click on "add expense" to add a new transaction.
  • Enter your transaction details.
  • Repeat until all transactions have been entered.
  • View existing groups in the "finances" menu.
  • To settle debts within a group, click on the "settle up" button.
  • Click on the dashboard buttons to access your financial overview.
